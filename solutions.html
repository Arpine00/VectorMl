Solutions

Vector ml Analytics Platform

Vector ml allows banks and lending institutions to manage ALM, credit risk, liquidity, pricing, and regulatory reporting all in one platform.

Treasury

Cash Flow Projections

  • Create and maintain accurate fixed-income cashflow projections. Calculate future principal and interest cashflows based on user-specified stress scenarios applied to interest, prepayment, default, recovery and delinquency rates as well as other more deal-specific assumptions.

Loan Valuation

  • Apply Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) at the loan level to value loan portfolios including tranches in securitizations and servicing rights, regardless of the lending vertical.

  • ‣ Development of asset-level cash flow assumptions
  • ‣ Development of expected cash flows
  • ‣ Development of discount rate assumptions
Asset Liability Management

  • With the integrated ALM (Asset Liability Management) feature, banks and lending institutions can monitor, manage and report interest rate risk and liquidity risk in one platform.

Liquidity Management

  • With traditional liquidity risk solutions, an analyst sometimes cannot even create and run LCR (Liquidity Coverage Ratio) analysis with custom specifications, which leads to some dependence on the IT department. Vector ML empowers analysts to quickly create and run LCR analysis after completing all the workflow steps.

Deposit Managment
  • ‣ Project future funding needs by tracking deposit maturity and cash-flow mismatches
  • ‣ Define the relevant maturities of the assets and liabilities
  • ‣ Plan large size funding operation in advance
Duration Gap Analysis

  • Examine the sensitivity of the market value of the financial institution’s to changes in interest rates.

Balance Sheet Modelling

  • Create an optimal and dynamic balance sheet structure by incorporating credit and liquidity risks for your organization.

Prepayment Analysis

  • Develop loan prepayment scenarios and create accurate forecasts using a broad range of economic variables and dynamic modeling assumptions.

VaR and Scenario Analysis

  • Banks need VaR to report regulatory capital usage under the Market Risk Rule. Vector ML helps you report risk across multiple asset classes using numerous approaches. Users can generate custom views of risk in a highly flexible environment.
